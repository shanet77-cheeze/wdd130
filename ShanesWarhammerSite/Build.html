When you pick up Warhammer 40k as a hobby, half the fun is painting your models! After choosing the faction and army you want to build, you will buy the models for your army created by Games Workshop. I recommend getting the models and army coolest to you! When you buy the models they will be gray pieces that you must assemble and paint yourself. You can choose to paint your models to reflect the lore accurate depictions from the 40k lore, or you can paint them however you want! Put your own creative spin on the models and flourish your creativity! There are many resources and videos out there to help you learn to paint your miniatures. Have fun painting and may the Emperor guide your hand!
