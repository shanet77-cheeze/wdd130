In the grim darkness of the far future, there is only war.

Learn about the awesome hobby that is Warhammer 40k. A tabletop war game created by Games Workshop in 1987. It is a sci fi spin off of a previous game Warhammer Fantasy. Warhammer 40k has grown into an expansive tabletop game, and goes beyond the tabletop with insanely expansive lore, books, video games, and soon to be tv shows and movies. There is a lot to enjoy with Warhammer 40k, whether it's painting the miniatures to bring your very own 40k army to life to battle against other players, or enjoying the rich 40k lore through the books, artworks and videogames within the 40k universe.

LORE: Learn about the factions fighting for dominance and survival in the grimdark future of 40k.

Build & Paint your Army: The hobby of 40k revolves around buying miniature models from Games Workshop, assembling the models, and painting them. Learn about the techniques to add a rugged realism to your miniatures and get inspired to build your own Warhammer army!
